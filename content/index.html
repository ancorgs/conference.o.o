About

From 19-22 July, 2013 the openSUSE Conference (oSC13) will bring together a wide variety of Open Source contributors to collaborate on one of the major Linux distributions projects. In four exciting days of talks, workshops and social gatherings we will bring alive our slogan "Power to the Geeko".
